OUR VENUE

Come and discover the romantic elegance of the Kyle House located in historic Fincastle. The Kyle House is an 1830 Victorian House with a wealth of history. Many local residents still refer to the property as the Old Bolton Store.

UNIQUE EXPERIENCE

The Kyle House is totally unique. Your guests will enjoy not being restricted to four walls. Your guests will have the opportunity to explore the whole property! Typically your reception starts in the downstairs old store and then your guests will meander upstairs to the banquet room to eat and then head back downstairs again for dancing. The house includes two beautiful parlor rooms, exquisite fireplaces (imagine getting married in front of the fireplace in the winter), a grand foyer, bridal dressing room, balcony with covered bar and seating area and a 60’ by 60’ garden with water fountain and trellis leading to the barn wedding chapel (perfect for wedding photos)

When you rent the Kyle House, it includes: tables, chiavari chairs, beautiful linens, china flatware, glassware and centerpieces, making it one of the most affordable venues to rent in the area. Be sure to make an appointment to see it.
